My UX Skills

User Research

Research can add depth to my projects by allowing me to empathize with the user and look at things from their persepctive.

User Personas

With all of the data gathered, I can craft together a user persona that represents who the target audience may be for a business or organization.

Problem Solving

Analyzing the findings from the research, with empathy and the user in mind, brings depth to any project and allows me to find the best possible solution for that need.

Wireframing

Wireframing provides the "skeleton" of my projects, which allows me to visualize and produce an end product that will both meet the needs of the user and stakeholder.

Storytelling

Good communcation is key to good design because it effectively conveys the "why" behind the product or service, and is the avenue in which listeners grab a hold of the mission and want to make it their own.
